About the Project

FlowZero is a cloud-native pipeline that uses satellite imagery to monitor river flow in under-observed regions, helping us conserve water and protect ecosystems. It's built on open-source tools, Planet API, and modern geospatial analysis techniques.

Monitoring Rivers from Space

How It Works

  • Satellite imagery retrieved from Planet Labs APIs
  • NDWI and spectral analysis to detect water presence
  • Edge detection and thresholding for flow estimation
  • Outputs include visualizations, CSVs, and logs
