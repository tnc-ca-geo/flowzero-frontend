Core Features

Discover the powerful capabilities that make FlowZero an innovative solution for water management.

NDWI-based Water Detection

Accurately identify water bodies using the Normalized Difference Water Index (NDWI) from multi-spectral satellite imagery, providing reliable data even in remote areas.

Automated Image Processing

Streamlined pipeline for retrieving, preprocessing, and analyzing satellite imagery with minimal human intervention, ensuring consistent and reliable results.

Binary Water Masks

Generate precise water extent maps through advanced thresholding techniques, enabling accurate flow estimation and change detection over time.

Time-series Visualizations

Interactive temporal analysis tools to monitor water level changes, seasonal variations, and long-term trends for informed decision-making.

CLI Tool

Powerful command-line interface that ensures reproducibility and allows for easy integration into existing workflows and systems.

Cloud Infrastructure

Built on AWS S3 and Planet API for scalable, efficient processing of large datasets across multiple watersheds simultaneously.

About FlowZero

FlowZero was developed at the University of San Francisco in collaboration with The Nature Conservancy to address the growing challenges of water resource management in the face of climate change.

Traditional stream gauges, while accurate, are expensive to deploy and maintain, leaving many critical waterways unmonitored. FlowZero bridges this gap by providing an open-source, cost-effective solution that leverages the power of satellite imagery and cloud computing.

Our mission is to democratize access to water monitoring data, enabling communities, researchers, and conservation organizations to make informed decisions about water resource management, flood prediction, and ecosystem protection.

Our Methodology

Discover the science and technology behind FlowZero's innovative approach to water monitoring.

1

Satellite Imagery Acquisition

FlowZero automatically queries and downloads high-resolution multi-spectral imagery from Planet Labs' satellite constellation through their API. The system is programmed to retrieve images at regular intervals or in response to specific events like rainfall or snowmelt.

2

NDWI Calculation

The Normalized Difference Water Index (NDWI) is calculated using the green and near-infrared (NIR) bands from the satellite imagery. This spectral index enhances the presence of water features while suppressing vegetation and soil signals.

3

Thresholding & Water Mask Generation

Advanced thresholding techniques like Otsu's method are applied to the NDWI images to separate water from non-water pixels. The result is a binary water mask that precisely delineates the water extent at the time of image capture.

4

Time Series Analysis

By processing images from different dates, FlowZero creates a time series of water extent measurements. This temporal data reveals patterns in water level fluctuations, seasonal variations, and long-term trends that can inform management decisions.

Command Line Interface

A powerful, user-friendly CLI tool for automating water monitoring workflows.

user@server:~$ flowzero --help

FlowZero CLI v1.0.0
A tool for monitoring water levels using satellite imagery

USAGE:
  flowzero [COMMAND] [OPTIONS]

COMMANDS:
  fetch       Download satellite imagery for a specific region and date range
  process     Process images using NDWI and generate water masks
  analyze     Perform time series analysis on processed data
  visualize   Create visualizations from analysis results
  help        Display help information for a command

user@server:~$ flowzero fetch --region cosumnes --start-date 2024-01-01 --end-date 2024-04-01

Fetching imagery for Cosumnes River region
Date range: 2024-01-01 to 2024-04-01
Connecting to Planet API...
Found 12 images matching criteria
Downloading: [====================] 100% Complete
Images saved to /data/cosumnes/raw/
Command Description Example
fetch Download satellite imagery for specified region and time period flowzero fetch --region cosumnes --start-date 2024-01-01
process Calculate NDWI and generate water masks from raw imagery flowzero process --input /data/raw --output /data/processed
analyze Perform statistical analysis on processed data flowzero analyze --input /data/processed --method timeseries
visualize Create plots, maps, and other visualizations flowzero visualize --type chart --data /data/analysis/results.csv
